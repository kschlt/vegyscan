   - What macro trends are driving market growth?
   - How is consumer behavior changing related to dietary restrictions?
   - What technology trends enable or impact this market?
   - Are there seasonal or cyclical patterns?

4. **Market Dynamics**
   - What are the key drivers for market growth?

   - What is the regulatory environment?
   - What external factors (economic, social, technological) influence the market?

5. **Customer Needs & Pain Points**
   - What problems do customers currently face?
   - How are customers solving these problems today?
   - What unmet needs exist?
   - What is the willingness to pay for solutions?
