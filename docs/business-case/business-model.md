## Research Framework

### Key Questions to Answer

1. **Value Creation**
   - What value does VegyScan create for users?
   - What problems do we solve and how?
   - What is our unique value proposition?

2. **Value Delivery**
   - How do we deliver value to users?
   - What channels do we use?
   - What customer relationships do we build?

3. **Value Capture (Revenue)**
   - How do we monetize?
   - What are users willing to pay for?
   - What pricing model optimizes revenue and adoption?

4. **Cost Structure**
   - What are our fixed and variable costs?
   - What are the key cost drivers?
   - How do costs scale with users?

5. **Unit Economics**
   - What is the Lifetime Value (LTV) of a customer?
   - What is the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)?
   - What is the LTV:CAC ratio?
   - What are the margins?
